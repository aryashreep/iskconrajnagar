+ ISKCON RAJNAGAR +

Welcome to ISKCON Rajnagar

ISKCON Rajnagar is dedicated to spreading the teachings of Lord Krishna and the values of the Bhagavad Gita. Our temple serves as a spiritual oasis for devotees and visitors, offering daily worship, festivals, prasadam distribution, and educational programs. We invite everyone to participate in our vibrant spiritual community and experience the joy of Krishna consciousness.

Our Temple

Located in Rajnagar, Odisha, our temple is home to the beautiful deities of Sri Sri Niladrinath Jagannath, Baladev, and Subhadra. We welcome devotees and guests for darshan, kirtan, and spiritual gatherings throughout the year.

Ministries & Activities

Our temple organizes regular Bhagavad Gita classes, youth programs, Food for Life prasadam distribution, Gou Seva (cow protection), and book distribution. Join us in our mission to serve society and share the timeless wisdom of Krishna consciousness.

  • Inauguration of ISKCON Rajnagar Temple

    24 Jan 2016

    On this auspicious day, ISKCON Rajnagar celebrated the grand installation of the deities of Sri Sri Niladrinath Jagannath, Baladev, and Subhadra. Devotees from across Odisha gathered for vibrant kirtans, abhishek ceremonies, and spiritual discourses. This event marked a new beginning for the temple, inviting everyone to participate in daily worship and experience the mercy of Lord Jagannath.

  • First Public Festival & Prasadam Distribution

    24 Jan 2016

    The day after the inauguration, ISKCON Rajnagar hosted its first major festival, welcoming hundreds of devotees and guests. The event featured joyous kirtans, enlightening discourses, and a grand prasadam feast distributed to all attendees. This marked the beginning of regular spiritual programs and community outreach at the temple.

+ OUR APPROACH

Our Approach to Spiritual Growth

ISKCON Rajnagar is committed to nurturing spiritual growth through devotion, education, and service. We follow the teachings of Lord Krishna and encourage everyone to participate in kirtan, study, and seva.

  • Ministry
  • Temple
  • Community

Our ministries include Bhagavad Gita classes, youth programs, Food for Life prasadam distribution, and Gou Seva. We aim to serve society and share Krishna's teachings with compassion and dedication.

The temple is a center for daily worship, spiritual gatherings, and festivals. We welcome everyone for darshan, kirtan, and to experience the vibrant spiritual atmosphere of ISKCON Rajnagar.

Our community is built on the principles of unity, service, and devotion. We encourage active participation in temple activities, volunteering, and spiritual study to foster personal and collective growth.
