+ FESTIVALS +

Sri Krishna Janmashtami

The divine appearance day of Lord Krishna, celebrated with abhisheka, kirtan, dramas, and prasadam distribution.

Donate for Janmashtami
Sri Radhashtami

Appearance day of Srimati Radharani, observed with special kirtans and devotional offerings.

Donate for Radhashtami
Gaura Purnima

Appearance day of Sri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu, celebrated with kirtan, discourses, and prasadam.

Donate for Gaura Purnima
Nrisimha Chaturdashi

Celebrating the appearance of Lord Narasimhadeva with pujas and devotional activities.

Donate for Nrisimha Chaturdashi
Sri Rama Navami

Appearance day of Lord Rama, celebrated with special pujas and kirtans.

Donate for Rama Navami
Ratha Yatra

The chariot festival of Lord Jagannath, Baladev, and Subhadra, featuring colorful processions and devotional singing.

Donate for Ratha Yatra
Govardhan Puja

Commemorating Lord Krishna’s lifting of Govardhan Hill, with Annakuta and special rituals.

Donate for Govardhan Puja
Gita Jayanti

The day Bhagavad Gita was spoken, celebrated with recitation and seminars.

Donate for Gita Jayanti
Jagannath Snana Yatra

Annual bathing festival of Lord Jagannath, Baladev, and Subhadra.

Donate for Snana Yatra
Jhulan Yatra

Swing festival of Lord Krishna and Srimati Radharani, celebrated with devotional singing and festivities.

Donate for Jhulan Yatra

All are welcome to join these festivals and experience the bliss of devotional service and spiritual culture at ISKCON Rajnagar.
